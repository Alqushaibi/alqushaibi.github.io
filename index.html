About Me

Currently serving as Program Coordinator for the Information Technology program at MAHSA University, with responsibilities in curriculum oversight, academic coordination, and quality assurance. A reliable and competent PhD graduate with over 8 years of combined experience in teaching, research and development, and student supervision, committed to academic excellence and innovation.

Expertise in Machine Learning, Deep Learning, and Data Science, backed by a strong publication record in Q1/Q2 journals and international conferences. My research spans Generative AI, Medical Image Processing, Metaheuristic Optimization, and Computer Vision, with work published in IEEE Access, Heliyon, Sustainability, and more.

Proven ability to design and deliver engaging curricula for courses including Advanced Databases, Data Science Fundamentals, and Structured Programming. Skilled in fostering student-centered learning through workshops such as Computer Vision with MATLAB and AI micro-credential programs.
